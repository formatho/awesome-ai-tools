## Hypothesis

**Statement:** Formatho can achieve first revenue within 30 days by establishing affiliate/referral partnerships with 3-5 top blockchain infrastructure providers.

**Prediction:** 
- Affiliate programs offer 10-70% commissions with 7-45 day cookie durations
- First revenue achievable within 30 days from partnership launch
- Integration time: 7-14 days to add referral links to existing tools/docs
- Expected conversion rate: 1-3% for developer tool referrals (based on Daily.dev benchmarks)

**Rationale:**
- Literature shows Binance (50% trading fee), Ledger (10-15%), Crypto.com ($50/referral)
- Developer tools like DevHunt (20% recurring), Coding Ninjas (30%), Figma (30%) demonstrate viability
- Affiliate model requires minimal integration work (just add referral links)
- Fastest path to revenue compared to deep integration partnerships (60-90 days)

## Experiment Design

### Scope
**In-Scope:**
- Identify affiliate programs for top 5 infrastructure providers
- Assess program terms (commission rates, cookie duration, payout minimums)
- Create integration plan for Formatho's existing tools/documentation
- Estimate revenue potential based on conservative assumptions

**Out-of-Scope:**
- Deep integrations with providers (save for H2)
- Privacy-first positioning analysis (save for H3)
- Ecosystem grant applications (save for H4)

### Target Providers
Based on CompareNodes market analysis:

1. **Alchemy** (90 protocols, 76 mainnets) - Market leader, likely has affiliate program
2. **Chainstack** (78 protocols, 80 mainnets) - From earlier research, privacy-conscious
3. **Ankr** (83 protocols, 77 mainnets) - Major multi-chain provider
4. **QuickNode** (market leader) - High visibility in developer community
5. **Tatum** (developer-focused API) - From Daily.dev research

### Success Criteria

**Primary Metric:** `partnership_feasibility_score`
- Score = (Number of viable affiliate programs found / 5) × 100
- Viable = Commission ≥ 15% AND Cookie duration ≥ 30 days

**Secondary Metrics:**
- Average commission rate across programs
- Average cookie duration across programs
- Estimated integration complexity (Low/Medium/High)
- Estimated time-to-first-revenue (days)

**Threshold for SUCCESS:**
- Partnership feasibility score ≥ 60% (3/5 providers viable)
- Average commission ≥ 20%
- Integration complexity = Low or Medium

## Risk Assessment

**Risks:**
1. **Program Restrictions:** Some providers may not have public affiliate programs
2. **Low Commissions:** Developer tools may have lower commissions than consumer products
3. **Short Cookie Durations:** May reduce conversion likelihood
4. **Compliance Requirements:** Some programs require approval or minimum traffic

**Mitigations:**
1. Target providers likely to have programs (market leaders)
2. Have backup list of 3 additional providers if top 5 don't work
3. Prioritize programs with long cookie durations (>30 days)
4. Prepare integration templates to speed up setup
