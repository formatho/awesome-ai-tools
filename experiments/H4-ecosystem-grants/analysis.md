# H4 Experiment Analysis: Ecosystem Grant Opportunities

## Executive Summary

**Status:** CONFIRMATORY - Ecosystem grants are viable for non-dilutive funding, but timing is uncertain and competitive.

Multiple L2 ecosystems have active grant programs, but timelines vary (90-180 days estimated). Base has the most structured ecosystem with multiple funding tracks.

## Key Findings

### 1. Base Ecosystem Programs: Multiple Paths to Funding

**Base Builder Grants (Ongoing)**
- **Program:** Small grants for builders with early ideas or initial prototypes
- **Criteria:**
  - Is builder creating something unique and fun?
  - Is builder bringing more users onchain?
  - Is builder's contribution live and making an impact?
- **Process:** Nomination-based via Google Form (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfXuEzmiAzRhie_z9raFCF1BXweXgVt18o-DvBuRRgyTygL2A/viewform)
- **Timing:** Ongoing, no application deadline
- **Funding:** Grant amount not specified (small grants based on description)
- **Fit for Formatho:** HIGH - Developer tools that bring users onchain qualify

**Base Batches: Startup Track (Structured Accelerator)**
- **Program:** 8-week virtual accelerator for pre-seed teams (<$250k raised)
- **Funding:** $10k grant to support teams during program
- **Investment:** Minimum 3 teams receive $50k investment from Base Ecosystem Fund (subject to diligence)
- **Structure:**
  - Virtual kick-off week
  - 8-week virtual program with dedicated advisor
  - Mentorship dashboard with subject matter experts
  - Interview series featured on Base TV
  - Demo Day in San Francisco (live audience of VCs, angels, accelerators)
- **Timeline:**
  - Applications: Feb 17 - Mar 9 (2026)
  - Virtual Program: Mar 23 - May 15
  - Demo Day: Late May
- **Fit for Formatho:** MEDIUM - Formatho is pre-seed with <$250k raised, qualifies for startup track
- **Constraint:** Application window closed (Mar 9), next cycle unknown

**Base Ecosystem Fund (Investment)**
- **Program:** Investment fund by Coinbase Ventures
- **Process:** Application via Google Form (authentication required)
- **Funding:** Investment amount not specified
- **Timeline:** Ongoing
- **Fit for Formatho:** UNKNOWN - Requires application form access (blocked by Google authentication)

**Paymaster Gas Credits**
- **Program:** Gas credits for developers via Coinbase Developer Platform
- **Link:** https://www.coinbase.com/developer-platform/products/paymaster
- **Funding:** Credits (not direct funding)
- **Fit for Formatho:** LOW - Gas credits reduce costs but don't provide capital

### 2. Base Developer Tools Ecosystem: Competitive but Opportunities Exist

**Base Ecosystem Developer Tools:**
- 0x - "Your one stop shop to build financial products on crypto rails"

## Strategic Assessment

### Strengths of Ecosystem Grants

1. **Non-Dilutive Funding:** Grants and investments do not require equity dilution
2. **Visibility + Funding:** Programs include marketing, Demo Days, mentorship
3. **Base Alignment:** Base is actively seeking developer tools and infrastructure builders
4. **Multiple Tracks:** Builder Grants (ongoing), Batches (accelerator), Ecosystem Fund (investment)

### Weaknesses/Constraints

1. **Uncertain Timing:** Builder Grants timing unknown (discovery process, no clear timeline)
2. **Application Windows:** Base Batches has specific application periods (Mar 9 closed for this cycle)
3. **Competitive:** All programs require selection process (nomination, interviews, due diligence)
4. **Not Fast Revenue:** Grants are non-dilutive but don't generate immediate revenue (30-day goal constraint)

## Conclusion

Ecosystem grants are a **viable but not fast** path to funding:

**Viable:**
- Multiple L2 ecosystems have active grant programs
- Base has structured funding with mentorship + visibility
- Non-dilutive funding available

**Not Fast:**
- Builder Grants timing unknown (no clear deadline or response time)
- Base Batches application window closed (next cycle unknown)
- Grant review and selection process takes time (90-180 days estimated)

**Complementary to Revenue Goals:**
- Ecosystem grants are best pursued **in parallel** with affiliate programs (H1) and integration partnerships (H2)
- Grants provide funding + visibility, but don't generate immediate revenue
- 30-day revenue goal is better served by affiliate programs (H1) + privacy positioning (H3)

**Key Strategic Question:** Should Formatho focus on Builder Grants (ongoing, low effort, uncertain outcome) or Base Batches (structured accelerator, next cycle unknown)?
