# Alchemy Startup Program Analysis

**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Source:** https://www.alchemy.com/startup-program
**Purpose:** Understand startup program for H2 hypothesis

## Program Overview

**Value Proposition:** "Ship faster on infrastructure built for scale. Alchemy credits and dedicated engineering support for qualified teams."

**Application URL:** Form at top of page (simple form, 30 seconds to complete)

## Program Benefits

### 1. Alchemy Credits
- Credits applied directly to account
- Offset infrastructure costs
- Extend runway
- Applied automatically to future invoices
- Works on pay-as-you-go or enterprise plans

### 2. $5,000 AWS Credits
- Compute, storage, databases on AWS
- Covered while finding product-market fit
- Significant value for early-stage startups

### 3. Dedicated Engineering Support
- 24/7 access to Alchemy engineering team
- Debugging assistance
- Architecture advice
- Hands-on help when critical

### 4. Partner Ecosystem Access
- VCs, accelerators, chain partners
- Introductions
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Launch support

### 5. Dedicated Onboarding
- Specialized onboarding experience
- Faster setup
- Better initial experience

## Application Process

### Form Requirements (Takes 30 seconds)
- Full name
- Email address
- Telegram (optional)
- Company name
- What you're building (optional)

### Process Steps
1. Fill out form (30 seconds)
2. Team reviews submission
3. Schedule a call to discuss
4. Credits hit account within days

### Quote from Zerion CEO
> "Working with Alchemy has helped us save equivalent of 3 full-time engineers, who otherwise would have to be heads down on infra maintenance at all times."
> - Evgeny Yurtaev, CEO & Co-founder, Zerion

## Strategic Considerations

### Pros
1. **Low effort application** - 30 seconds, minimal info required
2. **High value** - $5,000 AWS + credits + support
3. **Fast decision** - Days, not weeks
4. **Broad eligibility** - "Any team building onchain"
5. **Strong partner network** - VCs, accelerators for introductions

### Cons
1. **Must use Alchemy** - Credits only useful if using their infrastructure
2. **Competitive** - Many startups applying
3. **One-time benefit** - Not recurring (unlike referral)
4. **Call required** - May need to pitch during call

### For 30-Day Revenue Goal

**Direct revenue:** No - this is cost reduction, not revenue

**Indirect revenue potential:**
- Partner ecosystem introductions → potential funding
- Co-marketing → user acquisition
- Support reliability → faster shipping → faster revenue

**Assessment:** Less direct for 30-day revenue goal, but high strategic value
