**Quicknode-built integrations:**
- Clearly branded as "by Quicknode"
- Get "Quicknode Exclusive" badge

- Branded with provider name (0x Project, Covalent, Titan, Scorechain, Merkle)

## Key Insights

1. **AI category exists** - 🤖 AI Enabled badge suggests AI tools are welcome
2. **External providers succeed** - Covalent, 0x, Titan, Scorechain all have successful integrations
3. **Freemium works** - Many tools offer FREE tiers with paid upgrades
4. **Category matters** - Data & Analytics or potentially a new AI category for Formatho
5. **Badge game matters** - Staff Pick, Top Seller badges boost visibility
6. **Multi-chain is standard** - Most successful tools work across multiple chains

## Recommendations for Formatho

**Category placement:**
- Primary: Data & Analytics (most relevant)
- Badge target: 🤖 AI Enabled (natural fit)
- Secondary badges to pursue: Staff Pick, Top Seller

**Pricing strategy:**
- Offer FREE tier to drive adoption
- Paid tier around $49-$99/month (aligns with GoldRush at $50)
- Enterprise tier for multi-chain access

**Differentiation:**
- Focus on "Built for AI agents" angle (matches Covalent's description)
- Emphasize privacy-first positioning
- Highlight Formatho orchestration vs just data access

**Application approach:**
1. Apply as Technology Provider (not Service Provider)
2. Position as "AI-driven blockchain data and orchestration for developers"
3. Mention existing integrations and multi-chain support
4. Highlight privacy and developer experience
