# Blockchain Partnership Literature Survey

**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Status:** Initial Survey Complete

## Sources Analyzed
1. Infura (Consenys) - https://www.infura.io/
2. QuickNode - https://www.quicknode.com/
3. Alchemy - https://www.alchemy.com/

## Key Partnership Models Identified

### Model A: Marketplace Integration (QuickNode)
- Formal marketplace for partner tools and APIs
- One-click integration for customers
- Categories: Trading & DeFi, Security, Data & Analytics
- Clear discovery path for customers

### Model B: Referral Revenue Sharing (QuickNode)
- Revenue-sharing for customer referrals
- Trackable links and attribution
- Low overhead, scalable

### Model C: Startup Program Support (Alchemy, QuickNode)
- Credits and resources for early-stage companies
- Application process, limited spots
- Good for brand building and early customer acquisition

### Model D: Case Study Partnership (Alchemy, QuickNode)
- Featured customer stories
- Co-marketing and social proof
- High-impact, one-time effort

### Model E: Technical Integration (Infura, Alchemy, QuickNode)
- Build products on partner infrastructure
- API integrations
- Ecosystem distribution (e.g., MetaMask SDK, Consenys products)

### Model F: Enterprise Co-selling (Alchemy, QuickNode)
- Joint enterprise sales
- Combine solutions for enterprise customers
- Higher revenue, longer sales cycles

## Common Partnership Themes

### 1. **Freemium → Paid Conversion**
- All three offer free tiers to get started
- Partnership accelerates this conversion
- Usage-based pricing creates revenue upside

### 2. **Developer-Centric**
- Primary audience is developers
- Tools, SDKs, APIs are key integration points
- Documentation and onboarding critical

### 3. **Multi-Chain Support**
- Support for multiple blockchains is standard
- 80+ chains common (QuickNode)
- Partnership should work across chains

### 4. **Enterprise Focus**
- All three target enterprise customers
- SOC/ISO compliance common
- Dedicated infrastructure and SLAs available

### 5. **Ecosystem Thinking**
- Not just direct partnerships
- Integrate with partner products (MetaMask, Uniswap, etc.)
- Network effects matter

## Gaps Identified

6. **Co-marketing Budget** - Is there marketing support?

- Quick partnership setup
- Marketplace distribution
- Clear revenue model (referrals)

### Alchemy Seems Best For:
- Enterprise customers
- Larger deal sizes
- AI/blockchain intersection (natural fit)
- Strong brand association

### Infura Seems Best For:
- MetaMask/Consenys ecosystem access
