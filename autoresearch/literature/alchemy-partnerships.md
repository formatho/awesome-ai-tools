# Alchemy Partnership Analysis

**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Source:** https://www.alchemy.com/

## Overview
Alchemy is "the complete blockchain developer platform" powered by Cortex, an intelligent blockchain engine. They claim 13x more throughput and 5x more reliability than other providers.

## Key Products
- **Infrastructure:** Node RPC API, Rollups (RaaS)
- **Wallets:** Embedded wallets, Server wallets
- **Orchestration:** Gasless transactions, Stablecoins, DeFi APIs
- **Data:** Webhooks, Portfolio APIs, Token APIs
- **Devtools:** Debugging features, 24/7/365 support

## Partnership Structure

### 1. **Startup Program**
- Explicit program for early-stage companies
- Funding support (Solana $20M fund mentioned)
- Credit support up to $25k per team
- Application process

### 2. **Customer Story/Case Study Model**
- Featured case studies with major companies
- "Customer Stories" section dedicated to partnerships
- Co-marketing through case studies

### 3. **Ecosystem Integrations**
- Deep integrations with major platforms (Visa, Circle, World, Polymarket, Stripe, Robinhood, Uniswap, OpenSea, Chainlink)
- These are more customer relationships than formal partnerships

## Market Positioning
