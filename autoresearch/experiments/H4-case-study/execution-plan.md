Created: 2026-04-22
Status: Ready for execution when Formatho access is available

## Overview

This execution plan provides a step-by-step guide for implementing H4 (Case Study Partnership) from start to finish. The plan is organized by phases with clear deliverables, timelines, and success criteria.

**Goal:** Generate ≥3 inbound leads within 14 days of case study publication

**Total Timeline:** 6-8 weeks from first outreach to published case study

---

## Phase 1: Preparation (Week 1)

**Objective:** Get ready for outreach with all materials and research complete

### Week 1 Tasks

**Day 1: Finalize Outreach Materials**
- [ ] Review and customize all outreach templates (outreach-templates.md)
- [ ] Create partner-specific variants for Tier 1 partners (QuickNode, Alchemy, LangChain, Covalent, Pinecone)
- [ ] Set up email tracking (open rates, click rates, response rates)
- [ ] Prepare email signature with Formatho branding

**Day 2: Finalize Legal Materials**
- [ ] Review partnership agreement template (partnership-agreement-template.md)
- [ ] Decide: formal agreement or email-based for each partner?
- [ ] Prepare email agreement for quick partnerships
- [ ] Prepare formal agreement for enterprise partners

**Day 3: Finalize Case Study Materials**
- [ ] Review case study template (case-study-template.md)
- [ ] Create 5 case study concepts (one for each Tier 1 partner)
- [ ] Prepare architecture diagrams (ASCII or image files)
- [ ] Prepare code snippets for each concept

**Day 4: Partner Research Deep Dive**
- [ ] Research contact people for each Tier 1 partner
  - [ ] QuickNode: Head of Partnerships, Developer Relations, Marketing
  - [ ] Alchemy: Startup Program lead, Developer Relations
  - [ ] LangChain: Founder, Developer Relations
  - [ ] Covalent: Head of Growth, Developer Relations
  - [ ] Pinecone: Developer Relations, Partnerships
- [ ] Find their LinkedIn, Twitter, email addresses
- [ ] Research their recent content (blog posts, tweets, announcements)
- [ ] Identify mutual connections (if any)

**Day 5: Tracking Setup**
- [ ] Set up Google Analytics for Formatho website (if not already done)
- [ ] Create UTM parameter structure
- [ ] Create tracking spreadsheet (Google Sheets or Airtable)
- [ ] Prepare lead capture form on Formatho website (if not already done)
- [ ] Test tracking with sample links

**Deliverables by End of Week 1:**
- ✅ Outreach templates customized and ready
- ✅ Partnership agreements ready (formal and email-based)
- ✅ 5 case study concepts created
- ✅ Contact research for Tier 1 partners complete
- ✅ Tracking infrastructure set up

## Phase 3: Agreement & Planning (Week 4)

**Objective:** Finalize partnership agreements and create detailed case study plan

**Assumption:** At least 1 partner agreed to proceed

### Week 4 Tasks

**Day 1: Send Partnership Agreement**
- [ ] Send formal or email-based agreement to partner
- [ ] Include: scope, timeline, roles, benefits, tracking
- [ ] Set deadline for signature: 3 days

**Day 2-3: Schedule Case Study Planning Call**
- [ ] 30-60 min call with partner
- [ ] Agenda:
  - Case study angle and story
  - Customer story: real or fictional?
  - Technical details to include
  - Distribution channels
  - Timeline and milestones
  - Tracking and attribution

**Day 4: Create Detailed Case Study Outline**
- [ ] Based on planning call, create outline using case-study-template.md
- [ ] Include: title, customer, metrics, architecture, code snippets
- [ ] Share with partner for feedback
- [ ] Set deadline for approval: 2 days

**Day 5: Approval and Kickoff**
- [ ] Receive partner approval on outline
- [ ] Confirm distribution channels and timeline
- [ ] Set next milestone: First draft by [Date]

**Deliverables by End of Week 4:**
- ✅ Partnership agreement signed
- ✅ Case study outline approved
- ✅ Timeline confirmed with partner
- ✅ Distribution plan finalized

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk 1: Partner Doesn't Respond**

**Mitigation:**
- Send follow-ups (Day 3, Day 10, Day 20)
- Try multiple channels (email, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Expand to Tier 2 partners
- Create fictional case studies as backup

**Risk 2: Partner Responds But Doesn't Want to Collaborate**

**Mitigation:**
- Ask for feedback on why (learn from it)
- Refine value proposition and try again
- Ask for referrals to other potential partners
- Proceed with fictional case studies

**Risk 3: Partner Agrees But Takes Forever to Approve Content**

**Mitigation:**
- Set clear deadlines in agreement
- Follow up regularly
- Offer to do more work (they approve, we write/edit)
- If stuck, move on and try another partner

**Risk 4: Case Study Published But No Traffic/Leads**

**Mitigation:**
- Double-check UTM tracking
- Boost distribution (more channels, paid promotion)
- Improve CTA and landing page
- Analyze what went wrong and iterate

**Risk 5: Case Study Performs But Leads Are Low Quality**

**Mitigation:**
- Improve lead capture form (qualify leads better)
- Add friction (email gate for PDF download)
- Target different distribution channels (enterprise-focused)
- Adjust CTA (focus on free tier vs. enterprise demo)
