**Partner:** [Partner Name] ("Partner")

## 1. Purpose

Partner and Formatho agree to collaborate on the creation and publication of a joint case study highlighting the use of both products together. The case study will showcase technical implementation, use cases, and measurable results.

---

## 2. Scope of Work

### 2.1 Deliverables

**Formatho shall:**
- Research and write the case study (1,500-2,000 words)
- Create visual assets (architecture diagrams, charts)
- Provide code examples and technical details
- Format for multiple publication channels (blog, PDF)
- Design promotional graphics for social media
- Set up UTM tracking for lead attribution
- Distribute across agreed-upon channels
- Provide analytics report within 14 days of publication

**Partner shall:**
- Provide logo in high-resolution format (PNG/SVG)
- Review draft for technical accuracy (1-2 hours)
- Approve final version before publication
- Optional: Provide 1-2 sentence quote from a team member
- Publish or link to case study on Partner's channels (blog, newsletter, social media)

### 2.2 Content Ownership

- **Formatho** owns the written content, code examples, and visual assets created for this project.
- **Partner** retains ownership of their brand, logos, and any Partner-specific intellectual property included.
- Both parties grant each other a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, and distribute the case study for marketing and promotional purposes.

### 2.3 Publication Rights

- Both parties may publish the case study on their respective blogs, websites, and social media channels.
- Both parties may include the case study in marketing materials, presentations, and sales collateral.
- Formatho may also publish on third-party developer platforms (Dev.to, Medium, Reddit, Hacker News) with attribution to Partner.

## 4. Approval Process

### 4.1 Draft Review

- Formatho will submit draft outline to Partner for review and approval before writing begins.
- Formatho will submit first draft to Partner for review and feedback.
- Partner will provide feedback within 5 business days of receipt.
- Formatho will incorporate Partner's feedback and submit final version.
- Partner must approve final version in writing (email sufficient) before publication.

### 4.2 Approval Criteria

Partner may request changes if:
- Technical details are inaccurate
- Brand positioning is misrepresented
- Factual errors exist
- Compliance/legal concerns arise

Formatho will make reasonable efforts to accommodate all feedback within scope.

## 5. Branding and Attribution

### 5.1 Brand Guidelines

- Both parties' logos and brand assets will be displayed prominently and in accordance with each party's brand guidelines.
- Neither party will make false or misleading claims about the other's products.
- Both parties will maintain professional and respectful tone throughout.

### 5.2 Attribution

- Formatho will attribute Partner as a collaborator in all publications.
- Partner will attribute Formatho as a collaborator in all publications.
- Both parties will cross-link to each other's websites and case study.

Partner agrees to use these UTM parameters when linking to Formatho's version of the case study.

## Appendix A: Case Study Outline

**Working Title:** "[Title]"

**Structure:**
1. Introduction (150 words)
   - Problem statement
   - Why this matters to developers

2. The Challenge (300 words)
   - Specific pain point (15-20%)
   - Existing solutions and their limitations
   - What wasn't working

3. The Solution (800 words)
   - Architecture overview (40-50%)
   - How [Partner Name] + Formatho work together
   - Implementation details with code examples
   - Key technical decisions

4. The Results (450 words)
   - Quantitative metrics (30-40%)
   - Before/after comparisons
   - Cost savings, time savings, performance improvements
   - Lessons learned

5. Conclusion (100 words)
   - Summary and key takeaways
   - Call to action (try both products)

**Visual Elements:**
- Architecture diagram
- Code snippet screenshots
- Performance charts (before/after)
- [Partner Name] and Formatho logos

**Length:** 1,800-2,000 words
**Format:** Markdown (for blog) + PDF (for download)
