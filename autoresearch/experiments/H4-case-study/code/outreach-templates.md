## Response Handling

### If they say YES:

- Reply with Template 5 (Agreement Confirmation)
- Schedule kick-off call (15-30 min)
- Set timeline and deliverables
- Get their approval on draft outline
- Start writing immediately

### If they say MAYBE:

- Ask what specific concerns they have
- Address concerns with relevant info
- Offer to start small (one paragraph vs full case study)
- Emphasize zero-cost and minimal time commitment
- Offer references from other partners

### If they say NO:

- Thank them for their time
- Ask if they'd be open to future collaboration
- Keep door open for other types of partnerships
- Add to "re-contact in 6 months" list

### If they don't respond:

- Continue sequence (up to 7 touches)
- Vary the angle (benefits, timeline, examples)
- Try different channels (email, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- After 60 days, mark as "not interested now" and re-engage in 6 months

---

## Tips for Higher Response Rates

1. **Personalize everything** - Reference recent content, achievements, or posts
2. **Keep it short** - Under 200 words for initial outreach
3. **Lead with value** - What's in it for them, not you
4. **Show you've done homework** - Mention specific details about their product
5. **Make it easy to say yes** - Low friction, clear ask, minimal commitment
6. **Use social proof** - Mention other partners if you have them
7. **Follow up persistently but respectfully** - 7 touches over 60 days
8. **Test subject lines** - A/B test different approaches
9. **Time it right** - Tuesday-Thursday mornings, avoid Mondays and Fridays
10. **Be authentic** - Don't sound like a sales bot
