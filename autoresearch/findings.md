1. **QuickNode has the most structured partnership program**

   - Marketplace Integration (QuickNode)
   - Referral Revenue Sharing (QuickNode)
   - Startup Program Credits (Alchemy, QuickNode)
   - Case Study/Co-marketing (all three)
   - Technical Integration (all three)
   - Enterprise Co-selling (Alchemy, QuickNode)
